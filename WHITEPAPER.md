## The Innovation: Three Economies, One System

### Layer 1: Production (The Sim Game)
Players claim Martian territories and build resource extraction facilities in a stunning isometric 3D environment. Mine iron, extract water, harvest energy—each facility produces real, tradeable commodities. This isn't cosmetic gameplay; production directly feeds the financial layers below.

**Key Mechanic:** Every building is an asset. Every resource has real market value. Your ice extractor doesn't just look cool—it generates 240 H₂O daily, worth real SOL.

### Layer 2: Capital Markets (IPOs & Trading)
Here's where it gets revolutionary: **every business becomes a tradeable SPL token**. Built a profitable water company? Launch it as an IPO on a bonding curve. Raise capital, share profits, create liquidity.

**The Genius:** Every trade on a company's token generates fees that flow directly to token holders as resource dividends. A $MWTR token trades? 3% fee automatically buys water from the market and distributes it proportionally to all holders. You earn yield simply by holding shares in actively-traded companies.

This creates a self-sustaining flywheel: more trading → more fees → more resource dividends → higher token value → more trading. The yield isn't inflationary farming—it's **revenue sharing from actual market activity**. 

**The Builder Alignment Mechanism:** Company founders typically hold 60-70% of tokens via vesting schedules. If they abandon their company, token trading dies, their vested tokens become worthless, and they're vulnerable to hostile takeover. This forces long-term commitment—or they lose everything to aggressive acquirers. It's capitalism's ultimate accountability system.

### Layer 3: Speculation (Prediction Markets)
Every event, price movement, and corporate action spawns prediction markets. Will $MWTR hit $0.25 by Friday? Will the hostile takeover succeed? Which region gets hit by a meteor?

Speculators never need to build anything—they just bet on the chaos builders create. Market creators earn fees. Traders provide liquidity. Information asymmetry becomes monetizable edge.

## The Technical Architecture

Built on Solana for sub-second finality and minimal fees. Smart contracts handle all financial rails—IPOs, trading, settlements, distributions. The game state lives off-chain for smooth 60fps gameplay, with periodic checkpoints on-chain for security.

**Key Innovation:** Hybrid architecture means gamers experience zero blockchain friction while traders get instant settlement. Best of both worlds.

Core contracts: TerritoryRegistry (NFT land), CompanyFactory (IPO engine), ProductionEngine (yield calculations), ResourceMarket (order book DEX), TreasuryManager (automatic buybacks), PredictionMarket (AMM-based betting).
